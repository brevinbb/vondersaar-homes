01 / ABOUT

Thirty years of homes that could only be yours.

Vondersaar Homes is a family-owned builder with three decades of experience shaping custom, high-quality, eco-friendly homes. We don't work from a catalog of floor plans — we start with your vision and draw toward it, obsessing over the details most builders skip.

Whether it's your first home or your forever home, you work directly with the family behind the name. That's the difference you feel in every joint, every sightline, and every quiet, efficient corner of the finished house.
